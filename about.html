The Heritage

Crafted For Excellence

"Crafted for those who appreciate excellence."

Founded in the heart of historic Europe, Caffotto was born out of a desire to elevate coffee brewing into a fine art. Inspired by Milanese cafe culture and the absolute precision of Apple-level design, we craft sensory memories for our distinguished guests.

Our culinary vision unites traditional stone-oven cooking with contemporary French gastronomy. Every coffee bean is roasted in-house using custom fluid-bed roasters, highlighting delicate floral and stone fruit notes.

Culinary Vision

Michelin-starred standard preparation using premium imports from Naples and Alba.

Coffee Culture

100% Arabica Geisha and Bourbon micro-lots, brewed with water filtered to 150 TDS.
